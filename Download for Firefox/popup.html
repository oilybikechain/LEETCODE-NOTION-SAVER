Welcome to LeetNotion!

To start, download our Notion template and save (duplicate) it to your personal Notion workspace.

Duplicating ensures database compatibility.

Open & Duplicate Template

Create Integration

Go to your Notion Integrations page to create a new internal integration.

Check the following boxes:

  • Read content
  • Update content
  • Insert content
  • No user information

Get Database ID

Go to your duplicated template. Click the ... menu > Open as Full Page.

The ID is the 32-character code in the URL (between the slash / and the question mark ?).

⚠️ Don't forget to connect your Integration to the database! (Three dots > Connect to > Select your integration)
